This plugin displays the COVID-19 real-time data, top-headline news and job impact, quantitative geographical mapping and forecasting in the whole world and the United States through shortcodes like [covid19all] or [covid19usmap] in your WordPress post or page by using microservices from our own website https://us-coronavirus.info/. 

== Description ==
This plugin displays the COCID-19 real-time data, top-headline news and job impact, quantitative geographical mapping and forecasting through shortcodes like [covid19all] or [covid19usmap] in your WordPress post or page. The resultant post or page is mobile friendly. 

This plugin is free to use. Feel free to share https://us-coronavirus.info/ with your friends and family. We are glad to able to join Wordpress efforts alongside others in fighting against COVID-19.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload this plugin(unziped) to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place shortcodes in your post or page anywhere you want to show latest coronavirus data, news, maps or forecast.

== Shortcode ==

* `[covid19all]` shows everything we have, including real-time data, top-headline news and job impact, quantitative geographical mapping and forecasting. 

* `[covid19data]` shows real-time data for the whole world, the United States and all the counties in your state. 

* `[covid19news]` shows top-headline coronavirus news. 

* `[covid19job]` shows coronavirus job impact. 

* `[cov2019usmap]` shows a data map and a ranking of coronavirus in the United States with both state-level and county-level details.

* `[cov2019worldmap]` shows a data map and a ranking of coronavirus in the whole world.

* `[cov2019test]` shows a data map and a ranking of coronavirus test positive rate in the United States.

* `[covid19forecast]` shows United States coronavirus forecast in the next few days.
